Project 1 · Analytics-Ready Dataset

Project 2 · Market Risk & Volatility Forecasting
NVIDIA (NVDA) — GARCH(1,1) Volatility Pipeline
Completed

Estimated GARCH(1,1) volatility on NVIDIA daily log returns using ARIMA(2,0,3) as the mean equation. Produced a 30-day volatility outlook and prepared a professional investment risk memo. NVDA was classified as HIGH RISK with annualized volatility of 41.56% and a 30-day forecast of 49.19%.

03
Project 3 · Return Forecasting — ARIMA vs LSTM
NVIDIA (NVDA) — ARIMA vs LSTM Return Forecasting
Completed

Compared a classical ARIMA benchmark against a deep-learning LSTM network for forecasting NVIDIA daily log returns. Used chronological 70/15/15 train-validation-test split with no data leakage. ARIMA(1,0,0) was selected as the recommended model due to superior directional accuracy and negligible RMSE difference — confirming that LSTM complexity is not justified for this efficient large-cap equity.
